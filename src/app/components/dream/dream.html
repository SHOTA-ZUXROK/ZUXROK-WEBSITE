My Dream

My ultimate dream is to contribute to game development for the Fairy Tail series! I am currently learning game development in Unity. My skills are not yet as advanced or professional as required to even come close to working on game development for Fairy Tail; however, I am learning, and my dream is absolute! It will take a lot of time and dedication to achieve my dream, but the most valuable lesson the show has taught me is to never give up hope and never stop fighting.

This anime series has had some very nice games made for it. However, I believe there's potential for even more wonderful games to be created. Since I started watching Fairy Tail, I've always wanted to be involved with the series in my future. Realizing my passion for programming, I saw an opportunity to use my skills to contribute to the world of Fairy Tail.
